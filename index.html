Our aim was to create a robot with the maximum potential and minimize the cost.

In order to maximize the potential we used an Arduino and a Solderless-Breadboard -- which will allow for infinite amount of projects afterwards. The assembly is pieced together with hotglue, and the same wheels once on one robot can be removed then placed on a different chassis (bring around a bucket, a crayon box, Ice cooler, limited only to your imagination and the payload capacity of the motors).
